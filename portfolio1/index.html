About-Asta

Asta is a fictional character

Asta utilizes a rare ability called Anti-Magic, energy that cancels out mana. While Asta may not be able to cast any spells himself, he does have the unique ability to nullify the magic of others. Anti-Magic can be used to defend against harmful magic, and even cancel out effects like curses and magical trap
